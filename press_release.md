### New Open Dataset Published: Comprehensive longitudinal dataset (1995-2023) on poverty rates across 30 countries in Sub-Saharan Africa, South Asia, East Asia, Latin America, MENA, and Europe/Central Asia. Variables: poverty rates at $1.90, $3.20, and $5.50/day thresholds, extrem

**Universidad Internacional de La Rioja (UNIR)** — Juan Moisés de la Serna Tuya (ORCID: https://orcid.org/0000-0002-8401-8018) has published a new open dataset: *Comprehensive longitudinal dataset (1995-2023) on poverty rates across 30 countries in Sub-Saharan Africa, South Asia, East Asia, Latin America, MENA, and Europe/Central Asia. Variables: poverty rates at $1.90, $3.20, and $5.50/day thresholds, extrem*.

The dataset is freely available under the **CC0 1.0 Universal** license, allowing unrestricted use, distribution, and reproduction.
